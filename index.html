Level Scary: A Gorilla Tag Fan Game

by Aden Studios

Download

Level Scary is an immersive VR horror game inspired by the popular Gorilla Tag. Currently in beta, this fan game takes the familiar mechanics of Gorilla Tag and adds a terrifying twist.

Features

  • Playable Model: Experience the game with a detailed and responsive play model.
  • Working Mods: Enhance your gameplay with various mods that introduce new elements of fear.
  • Vent System: Navigate through a network of vents, adding to the suspenseful atmosphere.
  • Multiplayer Servers: Join friends or face the horror alone in a thrilling multiplayer setting.

Gameplay

Players navigate through dark, eerie environments filled with unexpected scares and challenging obstacles. The game features a detailed play model, working mods, and a network of vents that add to the suspenseful atmosphere.

Beta Phase

As Level Scary is currently in beta, players can experience the core gameplay while providing valuable feedback for future improvements.
